GUNS

 

Bond's classic gun, the Walther PPK is introduced in Dr. No when 007's Beretta is replaced by Major Boothroyd after a nasty incident when it jammed.

 

CARS

 

James Bond will forever be associated with Aston Martin, but 007 drove plenty of other interesting cars in the franchise's long history.

GONDOLA

 

The gondola  is similar to the gondola that was used in the Bond film "Moonraker". The first Gondola from Venice is  now in Sweden and may be viewed at the musum.

SWATCHES

 

The museum and the shop has the entire collection of James Bond watches featured in all Bond movies.

LIBRARY

 

The museum has a vast collection of James Bond books including Casino Royale Ian Fleming's first book published in 1959 by  Pearson with the help of James Bond himself.

CINEMA

 

Come and watch your favourite James Bond movie from the entire collection of movies from 1962-2015.

JAMES BOND BAR

A range of casino tables with low level lighting - including blackjack and roulette and the famous Bollinger champaign and vodka martini.

SHOP

The shop has the entire collection of  James Bond items featured in all James Bond movies including cars, planes, boats and swatches. They can also be purchased online.
